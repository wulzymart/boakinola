## About Us

## Brief History

Our firm B.O. Akinola was licensed by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria to practice on 24th May 1990 as a firm of Chartered Accountants in practice.

Before their commencement of private practice, each of the partners in the firm had accumulated several years of varied pre and post qualification experience at the highest levels in Business recovery, auditing, business accounting, finance and general management in reputable establishments and professional offices.

We render quality service to clients in various sectors of the economy such as Oil and Gas, Banking and Finance, Agriculture etc. as shown in the list of our clientele.
