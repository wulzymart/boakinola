This covers a wide range of services aimed at improving the efficiency and profitability either of an established or proposed company, for such purposes as expansion of existing product line or activity diversification into new ones, better competitiveness or production method, organization or re-organization of company structure.

Some of the more usual assignments under heading are:

- Feasibility Studies
- Investment analysis and appraisal
- Financial Analysis and Capital Structuring
- System Review, Control Evaluation and Data Processing
- Costing and Pricing Method
- Budgets and Budgetary Controls
- Management Information System
- Organisation Structure and Manpower Development
- Pension and Retirement Schemes
- Financial Advisory Services
